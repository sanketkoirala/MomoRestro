Our History

At Nepali Mono Pasal, our history is as rich and vibrant as the flavors we serve. Established in 2003, we embarked on a culinary journey to bring the essence of Nepal to our community. Inspired by the diverse and exquisite cuisine of Nepal, our founders set out to create a haven for food enthusiasts seeking an authentic taste experience. Over the years, we have become a beloved destination for both locals and visitors alike, who are captivated by the captivating aromas and delightful spices that define Nepali cuisine.With each dish meticulously crafted and infused with traditional techniques and secret family recipes, we take pride in preserving the culinary heritage of Nepal. As we continue to evolve and innovate, our commitment to delivering exceptional Nepali flavors remains unwavering, inviting you to join us on a gastronomic adventure through the heart of Nepal.

Our Staffs

Meet our incredible team at Nepali Mono Pasal, where we bring the vibrant flavors of Nepal to your plate. Led by our passionate and talented chefs, our kitchen is a hub of creativity and culinary expertise. With years of experience in Nepali cuisine, our chefs infuse every dish with authentic flavors and a touch of innovation. Behind the scenes, our dedicated waitstaff ensures that your dining experience is nothing short of exceptional, providing attentive service and warm hospitality. Together, our team works tirelessly to create a memorable dining experience that transports you to the rich cultural tapestry of Nepal.
